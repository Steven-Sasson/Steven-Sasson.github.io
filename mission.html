Who hasn't had crazy experiences?

We are a group of dedicated car and driving enthusiasts who truly care about people, driving, and road manners. Our goal is to make every driver connect with one another and with their vehicle in the same way racing drivers do.

Our mission is to be all-emcompassing just like car enthusiasts. Some enthusiasts focus on the type of car you drive, like import or domestic. Some people focus on design and style, while others don't care what we drive, as long as we drive! We brought our twin turbo V12s and our carbon fiber with us to fight the system that has tried putting us down for years

We aren't just for car enthusiasts of course. Anyone who;

      Wants to be a safer driver on the road.
      Wants to drive faster while staying out of trouble.
      Wants to connect with like-minded people.
      Wants to be a admired by the car community and other drivers.
Anyone can join!
